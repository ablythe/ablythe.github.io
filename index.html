About Me

Hey, I'm Alexander. Born and raised in Denver, Colorado, I've been in DC for eight years now. My interests skew towards the challenging and creative. Whether doing trivia and puzzles, hiking, or playing board games, I'm drawn to pursuits where the path isn't laid out for me. Which is why I love coding and the way it provides a unique and wonderful space wherein I get to think creatively and logically, collaborate with others, and where there's always a new challenge waiting around the corner.

I haven't always been on this path. I was an English major, who first picked up coding a couple of years ago. From the start, it has been an immensely rewarding experience that reminds me of everything I love about the liberal arts and humanities. From the critical thinking to the desire to better communicate through code, there are so many places where the two disciplines overlap and I feel as much at home sifting through lines of code as I do analyzing a novel.
